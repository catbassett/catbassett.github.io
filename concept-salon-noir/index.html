Experimental Direction

Salon Noir

This is the dramatic, gallery-like version. It treats Cat’s work as collectible and atmospheric, with a moody palette, richer contrast, and a much more luxurious tone than a typical freelance portfolio.

Best for: drama, mystique, luxury, confidence
What Makes It Interesting

Less ecommerce. More private view.

It frames Cat as a designer with taste, mood, and authorship. The work feels more intentional and less like a practical archive, which could be powerful if the audience is fashion-facing or premium.

01

Dark gallery mood

The background and typography create more emotional contrast and perceived sophistication.

02

Images as objects

Rather than flat tiles, the work is presented like curated pieces in a salon-style arrangement.

03

Personality through restraint

This version is bold by being unusually quiet, rich, and confident instead of loud.
