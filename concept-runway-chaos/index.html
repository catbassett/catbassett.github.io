Experimental Direction

Runway Chaos

This concept behaves more like a fashion campaign wall than a normal portfolio homepage. It feels urgent, graphic, layered, and a little unruly. The message is that Cat is not just organized and experienced, she is visually fearless.

Best for: a statement-making studio persona
Prints can shout
Senior designer energy
What Makes It Interesting

High fashion attitude, but still a portfolio.

Instead of behaving like a tidy menu, the homepage becomes a visual manifesto. Visitors would still get links into the main categories, but the emotional impact comes first.
